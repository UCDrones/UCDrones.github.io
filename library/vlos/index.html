Select Drone

Warning: Estimate based on visual acuity - actual range will depend on other factors including weather conditions and background clutter

The standard definition of normal visual acuity (20/20 vision) is the ability to resolve a spatial pattern separated by a visual angle of 1 arc minute (1/60 of a degree). This app calculates the size of the viewing plane of a drone (given the drone's horizontal and vertical dimensions) to find the distance that corresponds to 1 arc minute (maximum distance) and 3 arc minutes (safe distance) as a function of the drone's altitude.
